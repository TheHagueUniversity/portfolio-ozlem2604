LITERANATURE TYPEFACE

Literanature is a typeface which is designed by pieces of structures of Balat neighbourhood. Its name comes from literature and nature. It is designed by mixing pieces of nature and pieces of structures. I tried to combine a living thing with a human made thing. Actually, this typeface is a reaction to the destruction of nature.

While designing Literanature I took many photos in Balat, Ä°stanbul. I mostly preferred structures and nature photos. Then I combined parts from the photos in order to create the letters.
