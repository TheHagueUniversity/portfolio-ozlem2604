Chocolate Packagings

Melting Happiness is a café which claims to give happiness with their chocolates. The brand's motto is, "We don't sell chocolates, we give you happiness." It is a café that aims to bring every chocolate lovers together.

For this project, I created a café from scratch, I created the logo and the motto. I made three chocolates packaging which are Pumpkin flavored, Turkish coffee flavored and Dark chocolate. I experiment new flavores in the chocolates.
